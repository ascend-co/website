Entrepreneurial execution.

Acquisition Criteria

What we look for

EBITDA

$1MM – $5MM in annual EBITDA with healthy, stable margins

Recurring Revenue

Contract-based or repeat service models with predictable cash flow

Geography

U.S.-based businesses with regional density or expansion potential

Transition-Ready

Owners seeking succession, retirement, or a strategic partner

Established Operations

Proven track record with a loyal customer base and trained workforce

Roll-Up Potential

Fragmented competitive landscape with clear consolidation opportunity
